一、新模型名称（替代 Kinora）
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【推荐名称】ESKin

【英文全称】ESM-C and SMILES Transformer-based Kinetics Predictor
            （深度学习酶动力学预测模型）

【命名理由】
  - 风格对标 DLKcat（Deep Learning Kcat）、UniKP 等常见酶学 AI 命名
  - “Kinetics”涵盖 Km 与 kcat 两个动力学参数，体现多任务联合预测
  - 简短、易读、适合 Methods 标题与 GitHub 仓库名

【备选名称】（若老板有偏好可替换）
  - DeepKin   — Deep Kinetics network（更口语）
  - JESKin    — Joint Deep Learning Kinetics（强调联合预测，略长）
  - UniKin    — Unified Kinetics predictor（对标 UniKP）

【全文替换清单】（定名后需同步修改）
  □ docx 全文：Kinora → ESKin
  □ Abstract Keywords：Kinora → ESKin
  □ Methods 2.1 标题：Construction of the Kinora... → Construction of the ESKin...
  □ Figure 1 标题及图注
  □ README.md、GitHub 仓库说明
  □ 代码注释（config.py、train.py 等，可选）

【引用写法示例】
  首次出现：... we developed ESKin (ESM-C and SMILES Transformer-based Kinetics Predictor), a dual-input
  multitask deep learning framework ...
  此后简称：ESKin

四、修订后的 3.1 正文（英文，可直接粘贴进 docx）
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3.1 Construction and performance evaluation of the ESKin multitask prediction model

To enable joint prediction of the Michaelis constant (Km) and turnover number
(kcat) from enzyme–substrate pairs, we developed ESKin, a dual-input multitask
deep learning framework that accepts a protein sequence and a substrate SMILES
string as parallel inputs and predicts log10-transformed Km and kcat through two
task-specific output heads (Fig. 1A). ESKin was trained and evaluated under a
10-fold cross-validation strategy on a unified kinetics dataset of approximately
25,000 enzyme–substrate pairs, with fold assignments predefined by a 0.4
sequence-similarity split. Model optimization used a multitask Huber loss with
equal Km and kcat weights, AdamW optimization, gradient clipping, and early
stopping based on validation loss. Full architectural details and hyperparameters
are provided in Methods and in the GitHub repository.

ESKin exhibited stable convergence across folds, with decreasing multitask loss
and RMSE on the training partitions (Fig. 1B). The distributions of log10(kcat/Km)
were comparable across the 10 cross-validation folds (Fig. 1C), and predicted
values showed close agreement with experimental measurements in high-density
regions of the two-dimensional density map (Fig. 1D). Benchmarking against
published enzyme kinetics predictors on the same dataset and fold split, ESKin
achieved a Pearson correlation coefficient of 0.457 for kcat prediction,
outperforming DLKcat at 0.407 (Li et al., 2022) and UniKP at 0.440 (Yu et al.,
2023), while remaining below CataPro at 0.497 (Wang et al., 2025b). For Km
prediction, ESKin reached a PCC of 0.622, surpassing UniKP at 0.578 and
performing comparably to DLKcat at 0.627 and CataPro at 0.633 (Fig. 1E).
Per-fold test metrics and cross-validation summaries are reported in
Supplementary Table S1. These results establish ESKin as a competitive multitask
predictor for simultaneous Km and kcat estimation, supporting its application in
the downstream high-throughput enzyme screening workflow described in Section 3.2.

3.1 ESKin 多任务预测模型的构建与性能评估

为从酶–底物对联合预测 Km 与 kcat，我们开发了 ESKin——一个双输入多任务深度学习
框架，以蛋白质序列和底物 SMILES 为并行输入，通过双任务输出头预测 log10(Km)
与 log10(kcat)（图 1A）。模型在约 25,000 条酶–底物对的统一动力学数据集上，
按 0.4 序列相似性预定义的 fold 划分进行 10 折交叉验证训练与评估。优化策略包括
多任务 Huber 损失、AdamW 优化器、梯度裁剪及基于验证损失的早停。完整架构与超参数
详见 Methods 及 GitHub 仓库。

ESKin 在各折训练中收敛稳定，训练集多任务损失与 RMSE 持续下降（图 1B）。10 折
交叉验证中 log10(kcat/Km) 分布一致（图 1C），预测值与实验值在二维密度图高密度
区域吻合良好（图 1D）。在同一数据集与 fold 划分下与已发表方法对比，ESKin 对 kcat
预测的 PCC 为 0.457，优于 DLKcat（0.407）和 UniKP（0.440），低于 CataPro（0.497）；
对 Km 预测的 PCC 为 0.622，优于 UniKP（0.578），与 DLKcat（0.627）和 CataPro
（0.633）相当（图 1E）。逐折测试指标及交叉验证汇总见 Supplementary Table S1。
以上结果表明 ESKin 可作为 Km 与 kcat 联合预测的竞争性多任务模型，支撑后续高通量
酶筛选流程（3.2 节）。
